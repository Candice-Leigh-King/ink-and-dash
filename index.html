Hey!


Super happy you're here. I'm Catherine, but my nickname is Cassie and I often go by Cas. Tricky, I know.

Plain and simple, I write darn good copy to fit your brand. Writing content, whether it's for your website, blog or social media platform is all about making people care, right? It's about finding what inspires your audience while also delivering value.

Making people care can be accomplished through storytelling, well-written content and a variety of other techniques to sell your product, encourage people to explore your country or gain your first set of clients.

As a subject matter expert, I am able to curate premium content for whatever topic your brand requires. From longform articles and website SEO copywriting to creative stories and blog posts, I'm here to support your growing business.

To learn more about the different types of copywriting I offer, check out what I do.

My story


I'm a full-time freelance copywriter who also loves writing free-verse poems. In 2017, I graduated with a degree in journalism from Indiana University of PA and soon landed a job that allowed me to write for a diverse portfolio of brands, such as Ford and Caterpillar. I also work for The Hershey Company as their online retail content writer and blogger for the Hersheyland website.

Aside from writing, I am also a digital nomad wandering the world. I absolutely love exploring new cultures, countries, landscapes and tasting new foods! I'm slowly experiencing the world one country at a time. All I need is my laptop and good Wifi!

Want to know more? Follow my travel adventures at @catherineputt or poetry work at @ink_and_dash on Instagram.

What I Do
